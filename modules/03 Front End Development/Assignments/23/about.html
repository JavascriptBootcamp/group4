About Us

Pizza Prego started its way about 23 years ago, very quickly The pizza has gained a reputation and a lot of success thanks to its excellent pizza produced in a meticulous process of high quality dough, rich sauce (a private secret from Italian cuisine) and fine cheese. As a result of the great success, more branches were opened until it became a leading pizzerias chain.
Today, Prego Pizza is a nationwide chain: From the north: Binyamina, the entire Dan region, the coastal plain, Ashdod and the surrounding area, Modi'in and Jerusalem.
The Prego Pizza chain has regained its standard of uncompromising quality and service. The network is managed at the highest skill level with meticulous attention to the smallest details.
