Pizza Hut History

Pizza Hut is an American restaurant chain and international franchise founded in 1958 by Dan and Frank Carney. The company is known for its Italian-American cuisine menu, including pizza and pasta, as well as side dishes and desserts. Pizza Hut has 16,796 restaurants worldwide as of March 2018,[2] making it the world's largest pizza chain in terms of locations.
It is a subsidiary of Yum! Brands, Inc., one of the world's largest restaurant companies.
